## Hook Formats That Work

1. **The contradiction:** "Everyone says X. They're wrong."
2. **The list:** "10 lessons from [impressive thing]."
3. **The story:** "I lost $50K. Here's what I learned."
4. **The revelation:** "The real reason [thing] happens."
5. **The specific:** "Exactly how I [achieved result]."
